What am I doing here?
This initative Grannytell aims at connecting the younger generation and the older adults using stories and art. We want to hear your stories and questions about the older generation.
You can email or DM us on instagram with stories you’d like to share, or type questions in the box above (anonymous of course) and I will ask it for you!
Your question and response might be featured on our instagram page and final product!

Preview of my artwork:

I often wonder what advice I would have asked my grandparents if I were closer with them. I would like to think I am not the only one with this question, so this site provides the opportunity for you to ask the older generation any question.

Your questions will be added to mine and might feature in a book, which will have collections of stories about the older adults I know and their responses to our questions.
