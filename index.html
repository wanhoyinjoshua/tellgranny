My name is Joshua, I am 20 years old and I am a live-in student volunteer at a nursing home in NSW Australia. You can find me via wanhoyinjoshua@yahoo.com and my instagram is @laladrawdoodles.

What am I doing here?
I would like to publish a picture book called "What I have learnt living in a care home as a 20 year old". It is to document my volunteering experience but more importantly it gives me a reason to draw something because drawing is my passion.

I often wonder what advice I would have asked my grandparents if I were closer with them. I would like to think I am not the only one with this question, so this site provides the opportunity for you to ask the older generation any question.

Your questions will be added to mine and might feature in the book, which will have collections of stories about the older adults I know and their responses to our questions.

In order to thank you for your response, a digital copy of the end product (which might contain the response to your question) will be emailed to you upon completion if you would like to receive it. This is completely optional.
