My name is Joshua, I am 20 years old and I am a live-in student volunteer at a nursing home in NSW Australia. You can find me via goldsoulgrannytell@gmail.com and my instagram is @goldsoulgrannytell.

What am I doing here?
This initative Grannytell aims at connecting the younger generation and the older adults using stories and art.
If you have a story to tell the older adults you want to let the world knows, please email or DM us oninstagram., we will be more than happy to hear from you!
If you have a question for the older generation but am too afraid to ask? You give me the question by typing in the box above (anonymous of course) and I will ask it for you!
Your question and response might be featured on the instagram page!

Preview of my artwork:

I often wonder what advice I would have asked my grandparents if I were closer with them. I would like to think I am not the only one with this question, so this site provides the opportunity for you to ask the older generation any question.

Your questions will be added to mine and might feature in a book, which will have collections of stories about the older adults I know and their responses to our questions.

In order to thank you for your response, a digital copy of the end product (which might contain the response to your question) will be emailed to you upon completion if you would like to receive it. This is completely optional.
